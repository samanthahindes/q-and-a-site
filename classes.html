Not sure what classes to take? These are classes that have been very popular amongst WashU students in the past. Of course, you should take any classes that you find interesting and will be beneficial to you. The courses are broken down between the different schools and further by level of classes. For subject areas you are thinking about majoring in, you should take the introductory classes in your first two years.
