South 40

The South 40 is the best place for new students to live as freshmen and sophomores, with residential dorms, gym facilities, and a cafeteria. The South 40's buildings prove that WashU has the top housing in the country, with modern suites available to freshmen, and even tempurpedic mattress to ensure amazing sleep for all students.

Freshmen Buildings

  • Traditional Dorms: Lee, Beaumont
  • Modern Dorms: Koenig, Park, Lien, Umrath, Eliot A, Dardick

Sophomore Buildings

  • Traditional Suites: Hitzeman, Hurd, Myers
  • Modern Suites: Liggett, Mudd, Gregg, SoFoHo, Eliot B, Nemerov, Shepley Wheeler

The North Side

There are many other living options, both on campus and off campus.

  • The Village: Milbrook, Village House, Lopota House, Village East
  • Off Campus Apartments (North):The Lofts, Rosedale, UTerrace,
  • Off Campus Apartments (South): Greenway, UDrive, Kingsland, Wash Ave

For more information on any WashU housing options, visit the Residential Life website.


If WashU housing options aren't for you, many students choose to live off campus in Non-WashU apartments in numerous locations, such as Wash Ave, Kingsbury, Forsyth, or Pershing.
