Near Campus

No Car? No problem! There is a lot you can do in St. Louis that is walking distance from campus.

  • Forest Park- Just a 10 minute walk from campus gives you the most amazing park with tons of room for running or just relaxing. Sit on Art Hill with a book on a nice day to enjoy the weather!
  • The Loop- Take the overpass on campus straight to the Loop for excellent restaurants and shopping!
  • Clayton- the best area for restaurants near campus! See Where to Eat for Clayton restaurant recommendations.

Metro

Every semester each student can get a UPass, which gives you free transportation on the Metro. Use the metro to go straight downtown to take in a baseball game, visit the arch, or the famous adult playground called The City Museum. Visit Downtown STL for events and other fun activities!

A Bit Farther..

If you have a car at WashU, you can explore St. Louis a bit farther. There are many neighborhoods with fun restaurants, shopping, and other activities, such as Soulard, the Central West End, Clayton, and Towers Grove.
