There are many ways to get involved at WashU. The best way to find an organization that matches your interests is to attend the Activities Fair at the beginning of the Fall Semester.

Sports

  • Division 3 Sports: must be recruited, contact a coach if interested
  • Club Sports
    • 41 different teams
    • Vary in time commitment
    • Examples: tennis, crew, soccer
  • Intramural Sports
    • Fun way to get to know people through organized games
    • Examples: softball, basketball, volleyball

Clubs

WashU has a vide variety of clubs and organizations to capture the large array of interests of students. Clubs range from Academic teams, to community service organizations, to fashion magazines, to associations for religious groups. To find the perfect club for you, visit the Student Union Directory or attend the Activities Fair.

Greek Life

  • Women's Greek Life: must go through formal recruitment (See Panhel Website
  • Men's Greek Life: must go through rush (See IFC Website
  • Professional and Other Fraternities: must go through rush for individual organizations
